STUDENT LIFE AT THE ACADEMY

Lsport International Football Academy ensures player safety is essential. As a result, we employ a full-time qualified physiotherapist to help with football related injuries. Furthermore, private medical cover is offered to all players which ensures immediate attention to any serious injuries picked up at the academy. Please note this medical cover does not cover for any injuries picked up before joining the academy. Lsport International Football Academy also provides a Welfare Officer and all the residential staff hold the relevant DBS checks and child protection certificates. All students on the full-time programme are registered with a local GP and dentist whilst also remaining registered with their doctor back home for holiday periods.

STUDENT LIFE

Excursions

During your time at the Academy some optional excursions are arranged. In recent seasons, these have included trips to watch live football matches, cinema trips, swimming, paintballing, go-karting, team building, shopping, gyms, ten-pin bowling, Alton Towers and many other forms of entertainment. All of these excursions are fully supervised by Lsport Academy staff.

Weekends

Lsport International Football Academy is set-up for players to stay for seven days of the week however if they wish players are allowed to leave the academy at weekends with parental consent providing they return by a Sunday evening.

Meals

Lsport International Football Academy caters for three meals a day, seven days a week during the academic year. Over the years we have had many international students catering for different cuisines and any dietary requirements.

Religions

Many international students attend coming from a wide variety of cultures. The academy will always try to cater for religious requirements when possible.
