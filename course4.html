FOOTBALL DEVELOPMENT CAMPS 2018

Lsport International Football Academy are continuing their series of Development Camps for 2018. The Development Camp will combine coaching, guest speakers, nutritional advice and will culminate with a Showcase Game at Borderville Sports Centre in front of a panel of professional scouts. Players will stay at the Academy's Boarding House in yangon with training at the Borderville Sports Complex.


EVENT INFORMATION

Lsport International Football Academy have been running the Development Camps for over ten years. The camps have been designed for players aged 15 - 21 and give the ideal preparation and opportunity to develop all areas of their performance. The camps culminate in a final day showcase match, played in-front of a panel of invited scouts from professional and semi-professional clubs within the Myanmar.

The players will receive technical advice and day-to-day training sessions from LS's MLF A licensed coaches, as well as full-board accommodation at the Garden House. Each player will also receive an LSPORT t-shirt and complimentary copy of FourFourTwo magazine.

The boarding house will offer use of all its facilities on an exclusive basis - twin rooms with en-suite facilities, dining room, communal rooms, games room, study centre, conservatory and nearly an acre of walled gardens. WiFi is provided and our residential and welfare team are on hand to ensure that the students will have all they need to enjoy a safe, friendly stay at the Academy.
