FOOTBALL SHORT COURSES

Lsport International Football Academy also offer short courses designed to give players an experience of what life is like at the Academy. All short courses are fully residential using the same boarding house accommodation as the full-time Academy players, and optional extras such as English Language lessons can be provided.

COURSE INFORMATION

About Short Courses

The short courses offer intensive football coaching and training for football players aged 16-21 from the UK and abroad and will provide Match Analysis, Performance Analysis and Coaches advice. Short Courses work on a weekly basis, with playing paying for each week they stay.

Short course programme.

  • Included in a short course is:
    • Top quality football training at the The Borderville Sports Complex.
    • First-class football coaching programmes with UEFA 'A' coaches and advice from Managers in the professional game
    • An intensive football training regime from Monday - Saturday.
    • Fixtures against League and Non-League opposition with the opportunity to impress opponents and watching scouts
    • Specialist coaching and advice including strength & conditioning, diet & nutrition, goalkeeping coaching (if applicable)
    • Physiotherapy and sport injury cover.
    • Welfare support
    • Full board accommodation with 3 meals per day.
    • Recreational activities, excursions and Team-Building activities
    • Transportation to games.
