FULL-TIME FOOTBALL PROGRAMME

Our full-time programme runs from September to May. If the student is studying the BTEC Level 3 Extended Diploma in Sport (Performance and Excellence), the course lasts for two years. The academy's intensive training programme has been designed to enhance every individuals game. Our training sessions and matches are led by highly-qualified MLF A coaches, including experienced former professional players and managers. Sessions focus on improving individual technique, as well as developing tactical awareness and an understanding of the game. These sessions, alongside strengthening physical attributes through a robust conditioning programme delivered by Rhinos Gym, leaves the student fully prepared for the modern game.

COURSE INFORMATION

Academy Football Team

The Academy football team, MLF Yangon, are an YG Charter Standard Club, competing in the National League U19 AEYAWADEADY, Shan Colleges U19 League and Midland Football U21 League, as well as local county Ls tournaments. Friendly fixtures are regularly arranged against Premier League and Football League opposition, whilst specific showcase matches are staged in front of Premier League and Myanmar Football League scouts, giving each student a chance to test and benchmark their development against professional clubs in Yangon. Each year, subject to EU funding, students in their second year can attend a two-week placement with a professional club in Thaliand. Placements in the past have taken place in japan and China, enabling students to receive further training specific to Asia football, whilst enhancing opportunities of progression within the game.

Football Activities

  • Away from the pitch itself, a number of activities are arranged to further enhance the students football development. Examples of some of these can include:
    • Guest speaker sessions with former professional footballers and managers.
    • Sessions about contracts with FIFA-approved Player Agents.
    • Expert coaching sessions from UEFA-qualified coaches.
    • Specialist goalkeeping sessions from MLF-qualified coaches.
    • Filming of matches and post-match analysis.
    • Specific strength and conditioning sessions with qualified instructors
    • Fitness monitoring and testing
    • Membership to Rhino’s Elite strength and conditioning gym.
    • Physiotherapy sessions and first aid training.
    • Nutrition, health and lifestyle advice.
    • Team building activities.
    • Media Training.
    • Opportunity to take FA Coaching badges and referee’s course.
    • Support with exit pathways and provision of references.
    • FPrivate medical insurance (optional).
